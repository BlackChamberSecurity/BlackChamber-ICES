# Contributing to Black Chamber

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Black Chamber!

## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

All contributors are required to sign a Contributor License Agreement (CLA) before their contributions can be merged. This ensures that we have the legal right to distribute your contributions while you retain your copyright.

Please review the [CLA](CLA.md) file in this repository. When you open a Pull Request, our bot may ask you to sign the CLA if you haven't already.

## How to Contribute

1.  Fork the repository.
2.  Create a new branch for your feature or bug fix.
3.  Make your changes.
4.  Ensure your code passes all tests and linting checks.
5.  Submit a Pull Request.

We appreciate your help!
